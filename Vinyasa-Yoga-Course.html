Vinyasa yoga

Vinyasa Yoga is an energetic and fluid style of yoga where movement is synchronized with breath. Unlike slower-paced practices, Vinyasa offers a faster pace, linking each breath to a movement in a continuous flow. It's perfect for those seeking a more active and invigorating practice while still maintaining mindfulness and balance.

About the course

Hatha Yoga is one of the most traditional and widely practiced forms of yoga, known for its focus on posture (asana), breath control (pranayama), and deep awareness. It is ideal for those who wish to build a strong foundation in yoga, develop a deeper mind-body connection, and cultivate a calm, focused state of being.

More details

Unlike fast-paced yoga styles, Hatha moves at a slower rhythm, allowing time to settle into each pose and explore proper alignment. This mindful pace makes it accessible to beginners while still offering depth and challenge for more experienced students.

  • Steady, purposeful movement: Carefully guided sequences that emphasize strength, balance
  • Focused breathing techniques: Learn to control and deepen your breath to enhance energy
  • Postural alignment: Attention to form and technique helps prevent injury
  • Meditative awareness: Each session encourages mindfulness, helping you become more present
  • Stress relief: The combination of breath, movement, and stillness creates a deeply calming effect

How the Course Works

Our monthly yoga course is designed to keep your practice consistent, fresh, and evolving—all from the comfort of your home.

New Classes Published Progressively

Each month, new classes are released on Mondays & Fridays at the indicated time. You’ll get access to these classes as soon as they’re live. Each new class is designed to help you build progressively on your practice, with fresh themes and focuses every month.

On-Demand Access to Previously Released Classes

You can always go back and review previously released classes. If you miss a class or want to revisit a particular sequence, simply log in and access them anytime. However, you'll need to wait for the next class to be published on the scheduled dates.

Easy-to-Follow Schedule

Each class lasts about 45 minutes, and we recommend following the schedule to stay in sync with the flow of the course. But if you need flexibility, feel free to watch the videos whenever you’re ready—just know that new content is released at the scheduled times.

Progressive Learning

The monthly themes build on one another. As you progress, each class adds more complexity, helping you gain strength, flexibility, and mindfulness. You'll be guided through practices that expand your skill set while keeping you grounded in the fundamentals.

All You Need Is a Mat

No special equipment required—just a mat, internet connection, and your commitment to show up for yourself.

About us

Founded by passionate yoga practitioners, we’re dedicated to offering high-quality online classes that support your mental, physical, and emotional wellbeing. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced yogi, our diverse classes are designed to empower you to show up for yourself, wherever you are.

The values that guide us

At Pure Yoga Space, our practice goes beyond the mat. The values that guide us are at the heart of everything we do:

Lasting impact

Our goal is to help you create lasting change. Whether it’s improving flexibility, managing stress, or building strength, we aim to leave a positive, lasting impact on your life—physically, mentally, and emotionally.

Inclusive & empowering

Yoga is for everyone. We create a space where all individuals—regardless of experience, age, or background—can feel welcomed and supported. Through our practice, we aim to empower you to become the best version of yourself, inside and out.

Ongoing improvement

We are committed to continuous growth, both as a community and as individuals. We believe in the power of progress and encourage consistent learning, self-reflection, and improvement in all aspects of life.

Our wonderful team

At Pure Yoga Space, we are proud to have a team of passionate, experienced, and dedicated instructors who are committed to guiding you on your yoga journey. Each of our teachers brings a unique perspective, style, and energy to their classes, ensuring that you have a diverse and enriching experience.
